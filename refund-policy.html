Our Policy in Plain English

All Digital Sales Are Final

Because our products are digital and delivered instantly, we do not offer refunds after download. However, we stand behind our products — if something is broken or not as described, we will make it right.

Why We Have a No-Refund Policy

Unlike physical goods, digital products cannot be "returned" once downloaded. The moment a file is accessed, the value has been delivered. For this reason, all sales on Digi.Supply are final after the product has been accessed or downloaded.

We encourage all customers to review product descriptions, feature lists, and screenshots carefully before purchasing. If you have questions about a product before buying, email us at hello@digisupply.com and we'll answer promptly.

What We Will & Won't Refund

✓ We Will Refund If...

  • You were charged twice for the same order
  • The product file is corrupt and we cannot fix it
  • The product is significantly different from its description
  • You never received your download link and we cannot resend it
  • A technical error on our end prevented access

✗ We Will NOT Refund If...

  • You changed your mind after downloading
  • You found a cheaper version elsewhere
  • You didn't read the product description
  • The product didn't produce the income results you expected
  • You no longer need the product
  • More than 7 days have passed since purchase

Subscription Products

For subscription-based products like the Digital Product Factory:

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period
  • No partial refunds are issued for unused days in a billing period
  • You retain access until the end of your paid period after cancellation

How to Request a Refund

If you believe you qualify for a refund based on the criteria above:

  • Email hello@digisupply.com within 7 days of purchase
  • Include your order number and email address used at checkout
  • Describe the issue clearly
  • We will respond within 48 business hours
Approved refunds are processed back to your original payment method within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

Chargebacks

We take chargebacks seriously. If you initiate a chargeback without first contacting us to resolve the issue, we reserve the right to dispute the chargeback and provide Stripe with evidence of delivery and your agreement to these terms.

We always prefer to resolve issues directly — please reach out before disputing a charge.

Still Have Questions?

We're real people and we want you to be happy with your purchase. Reach out before you buy if you're unsure about anything.
